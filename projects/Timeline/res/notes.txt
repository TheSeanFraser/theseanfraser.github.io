-- Methodology based off Mike Burrell's blog post:
http://mikeburrell.blogspot.com/2012/04/guide-to-spring-arrival-dates-in.html

M = 0.2 X (P - W) + W

Where:
M = mass arrival date
P = peak arrival date
W = winter frequency
